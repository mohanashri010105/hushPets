Betta :

Bettas are anabantoids, which means they can breathe atmospheric air using a unique organ called the labyrinth

    Adult:
  • Height:10-16 in
  • Weight:18-22 lb
  • Length:19-40 in
  • Life:10 yrs

Gold Fish:

Goldfish have strong associative learning abilities, as well as social learning skills. In addition, their visual acuity allows them to distinguish between individual humans

    Adult:
  • Height:10-16 in
  • Weight:18-22 lb
  • Length:19-40 in
  • Life:12 yrs

Angel Fish:

Pterophyllum are unusually shaped for cichlids being greatly laterally compressed, with round bodies and elongated triangular dorsal and anal fins. This body shape allows them to hide among roots and plants, often on a vertical surface

    Adult:
  • Height:10-16 in
  • Weight:18-22 lb
  • Length:19-40 in
  • Life:12 yrs
